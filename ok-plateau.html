The plateau is invisible. You don't feel yourself stop improving. You just gradually become someone who is "pretty good" at something, and stay there, for years, sometimes forever.
Start small. Find one thing you do without thinking and make it slightly harder this week. Ten percent. That's the whole secret.
When it starts to feel uncomfortable,
that discomfort is not the problem.
That is the progress.
